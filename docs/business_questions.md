# Business Questions

This project answers common healthcare revenue cycle and financial reporting questions using Microsoft SQL Server.

---

## Patient & Provider Analysis

- How many unique patients exist in the database?
- How many providers submitted Medicare claims?
- How many providers are associated with each payer?
- How many physicians and patients are associated with each healthcare location?

---

## CPT Utilization

- Which CPT codes generated the highest procedure volume?
- Which CPT codes exceeded 100 total units?
- How many CPT units were associated with diagnosis codes beginning with the letter "J"?
- Which diagnosis groups generated the greatest procedure volume?

---

## Revenue Cycle Performance

- What is the Gross Collection Rate for each healthcare location?
- Which payer generated the highest total payments?
- Which locations demonstrated the strongest financial performance?

---

## Physician Specialty Analysis

- Which physician specialty received the highest payments?
- What were the monthly payment trends for Internal Medicine?
- How do physician specialties compare financially?

---

## Credentialing Analysis

- How much revenue was written off because of credentialing issues?
- Which healthcare location experienced the largest credentialing write-offs?
- Which physicians were affected by credentialing adjustments?
- What was the financial impact by physician?

---

## Patient Demographics

- What are the age distributions of patients?
- How are patients distributed by age group?
- What demographic information is available for reporting?

---

## Clinical Analysis

- What is the average age of patients diagnosed with Type 2 diabetes?
- How many patients with Type 2 diabetes were treated at Big Heart Community Hospital?
- How does patient age differ by gender?

---

## Charge & Payment Analysis

- What is the average charge per CPT unit?
- What is the average payment per CPT unit?
- How do payments differ across insurance payers?

---

## Financial Performance

- What is the Net Collection Rate by physician specialty?
- What percentage of net charges remains in Accounts Receivable?
- What percentage of revenue is written off?
- Which specialties demonstrate the strongest financial performance?

---

## Executive Reporting

- Which healthcare locations generated the highest gross charges?
- What is the average charge per patient?
- How many physicians support each healthcare location?
- Which locations serve the largest patient populations?
